# Regulatory Landscape Component

EU AI Act, US executive orders, NIST AI RMF requirements, state-level AI
laws — nobody has mapped which provisions specifically apply to agentic AI
vs. general ML vs. traditional software.

Framework-coverage evaluates technical standards. This evaluates legal and
regulatory obligations with the same rigor. Cross-reference obligations to
threats and mitigations where applicable.

Scope declarations matter here more than anywhere — "applies to organizations
deploying agents in the EU" vs. "does not cover sector-specific regulations."
Validity windows should be short (30-60 days) because this space moves fast.

High value because legal teams and security teams are currently guessing at
this mapping. No public resource normalizes it.
